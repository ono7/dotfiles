# use D&B to give depth and local contrast

you can also burn the edges of the shawdows to make them pop

1. option + layer icon `(looks line [+])` creates new layer, name it DD-Light
2. choose mode: overlay, linear dodge, soft light etc,
3. select fill with neutral color gray (50%) use x to change to black or white to take away (dodge) or add(burn)
4. command+j to copy, rename to DD-Dark (this will be the dark layer)
5. select fill with neutral color gray (50%)
